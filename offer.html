Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a mystery gift box?

No. My Seasonal Spark is a seasonal ecommerce catalog with named products, variants, and transparent prices.

Why does checkout route through Shopify?

B2 is a Shopify/POD ecommerce business. Shopify is the expected customer checkout and product-management surface.

Are all products ready for live ordering?

No live checkout should be treated as complete until the Shopify domain, payment, supplier binding, and fulfillment proof are verified.

Can products be personalized?

Some catalog items support names, photos, dates, or gift notes. Personalization is product-specific, not a generic bundle.
